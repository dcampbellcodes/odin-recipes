Protien Pancakes

While pancakes are not the most healthy breakfast food. A little tweaks to the age old recipe can make it a healthy and delicious meal.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rolled Oats
  • 1 Small Banana
  • 1/2 cup Egg Whites
  • 2 scoops Protein Powder
  • 2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Almond Milk

Steps

  1. Start by adding the ingredients into a blender in the order listed above.
  2. Blend the mixture until it is completely smooth and there are no chunks
  3. Be sure to scrape down the sides as needed.
  4. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes to allow it to thicken.
  5. Use a 1/4 cup to scoop and pour the batter onto a heated pan.
  6. Cook the pancake over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes on each side. Slowly cooking the pancakes will result in fluffy, fully cooked â€˜cakes!
  7. ENJOY!
