Chicken & Veggies

Another healthy chicked recipe!


Ingredients

  • 1 boneless and skinless chicken breast
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of frozen or fresh mixed vegetables
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of water to a small sauce pan and bring to a rolling boil. Once boiling add a pinch of salt and a tablespoon of butter to the water with the 1/2 cup of mixed vegetables. Bring back to a boil, cover and reduce to a simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  2. In a medium frying pan, lightly coat with oil. Bring up to medium heat. Seperately season the chicken to you desire, I like to rub it down with cruushed garlic and lemon pepper.
  3. Add the chicked to the frying pan, top of the breast first. Let cook for 4-5 minutes on medium heat. Flip when the top of the breast has reached a golden brown.
  4. Once the chicken is completely cooked the vegetables should be ready. Go ahead and plate the chicken.
  5. Mix the vegetables with two tablespoons of butter, salt and pepper.
  6. ENJOY!;
