Egg Salad

Eggs are probably the most versatile super food on the planet. You can fry them, boil them and so much more. However, one of my favorite ways to eat them is to make egg salad.


Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat.
  2. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
  4. ENJOY!
