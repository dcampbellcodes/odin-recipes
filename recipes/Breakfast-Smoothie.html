Breakfast Smoothie

While breakfast may or may not be the most important meal of the day. It is, in my opinion, the best way to start your day!


Ingredients

  • Blender
  • Frozen Berries
  • Milk
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Protein Powder
  • Super Food Powder

Steps

  1. Add one cup of frozen berries to the blender.
  2. Add half a cup of Greek Yogurt to the blender.
  3. Add half a cup of milk to your blender.
  4. Add one scoop of Protein Powder to the blender.
  5. Add one scoop of Super Food Powder to the blender.
  6. Blend until smooth.
  7. ENJOY!
